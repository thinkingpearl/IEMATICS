About us

IEMATICS is an online International Mathematics Olympiad organised in this year for the fourth time with pomp and splendour by the Society for Makers, Artists, Researchers and Technologists, (SMART), USA which is sponsored by Institute of Engineering & Management (IEM) in association with Society for Data Science(S4DS) student chapter,IEM ,Kolkata and Codechef. This Olympiad aims at developing technological and mathematical skills among tech-learners and other students of 10+2 and above. This competition is organised to put more emphasis on mathematical skills and its application rather than mere theory.

Date of event: 24th October, 2021
The competition will start from 06:00 pm IST on 24th October 2021 and will continue till 10:00 pm IST on 24th October 2021.
